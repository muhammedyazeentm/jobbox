JOBBOX - About Us

Connecting Local Talent with Local Opportunities

Welcome to JOBBOX, a platform built to simplify and strengthen local hiring. Our mission is to connect shops, businesses, and individuals with the right talent in their neighborhoods—no resumes, no complicated processes, just real people and real jobs.

What We Do

We help local shop owners, small businesses, and individuals find reliable workers for roles like sales assistants, chefs, delivery helpers, housekeepers, and more. Whether it’s a permanent, temporary, or part-time job, our platform allows employers to post vacancies, and job seekers to apply quickly with a skill-based profile.

Why Choose Us?

  • No Resume Required – We focus on skills and experience, not paperwork.
  • Quick Hiring – Post a job and start receiving applications within minutes.
  • Easy Communication – Directly message applicants and schedule interviews or trials.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted job platform for local employment, empowering small businesses and skilled workers by breaking down barriers to opportunity.

Who We Serve

  • Local shop owners
  • Home-based entrepreneurs
  • Small and medium-sized businesses
  • Individuals seeking quick or part-time jobs
